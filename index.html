How to Play Blackjack

  • Goal: Beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.
  • Card Values:
    • Numbered cards (2-10) are worth their face value.
    • Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points.
    • Aces (A) are worth 11 or 1 (the game automatically adjusts this to save you from busting).
  • Hit: Take another card to increase your score.
  • Stand: Keep your current cards and end your turn.
  • Dealer Rules: The dealer must continue to take cards ("hit") until their cards total 17 or higher.
  • Win/Loss: You win if your total is higher than the dealer's, or if the dealer "busts" (goes over 21). You lose if you go over 21 or if the dealer has a higher final score.
